[RETURNS]
Our return policy allows customers to return any item within 30 days of purchase.
Items must be unused and in original packaging.
To start a return, email support@company.com with your order number.
Refunds are processed within 5-7 business days after we receive the item.

[SHIPPING]
Standard shipping takes 5-7 business days.
Express shipping takes 1-2 business days and costs $12.99.
Free shipping is available on orders over $50.
We ship to all 50 US states and 30 international countries.
International orders may take 10-15 business days.

[ACCOUNT]
To reset your password, click "Forgot Password" on the login page.
Account email changes require verification from your old email address.
To delete your account, contact support@company.com — processed within 48 hours.
You can update billing information under Settings > Payment Methods.

[ORDERS]
You can track your order using the link in your confirmation email.
Orders can be cancelled within 2 hours of placement.
If your order hasn't arrived after 10 days, contact support with your order number.
We send order confirmation emails within 5 minutes of purchase.

[PAYMENTS]
We accept Visa, Mastercard, American Express, PayPal, and Apple Pay.
All transactions are encrypted with SSL security.
Payment issues? Contact your bank first, then reach us at support@company.com.
We do not store full credit card numbers on our servers.

[PRODUCT]
All products come with a 1-year manufacturer warranty.
Warranty claims require proof of purchase.
We release new products every quarter — subscribe to our newsletter for updates.
Custom orders are available for bulk purchases over 50 units.
